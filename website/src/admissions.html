Eligibility Requirements

Review the eligibility criteria for our undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

Undergraduate Programs (B.E./B.Tech)

  • 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks
  • Valid score in entrance examination
  • Age limit: 17-21 years

Postgraduate Programs (M.E./M.Tech)

  • Relevant Bachelor's degree in Engineering
  • Minimum 65% aggregate marks
  • Valid GATE score preferred
  • Department-specific requirements apply

MBA Program

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid CAT/MAT/CMAT score
  • Group discussion and personal interview

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer various merit-based and need-based scholarships to support deserving students.

Merit Scholarship

Awarded to students with outstanding academic performance in previous examinations.

  • Top 1% of applicants: 100% tuition fee waiver
  • Top 5% of applicants: 50% tuition fee waiver
  • Top 10% of applicants: 25% tuition fee waiver

Sports Scholarship

For students with exceptional achievements in sports at state/national level.

  • National level: Up to 100% tuition fee waiver
  • State level: Up to 50% tuition fee waiver
  • District level: Up to 25% tuition fee waiver

Need-Based Scholarship

Financial assistance for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Family income below ₹2.5 LPA: Up to 100%
  • Family income below ₹5 LPA: Up to 50%
  • Family income below ₹7.5 LPA: Up to 25%
